About Us

Learn more about who we are and how we work

Who We Are

At Found Home, we believe that finding the right space should be simple, secure, and stress-free. We are a trusted property rental and listing platform designed to help individuals and families rent, book, or even sell their homes with ease. Whether you're searching for a room to rent, a house to book for a stay, or looking to list your own property, Found Home brings everything under one reliable platform. Our system is built for convenience — with verified listings, direct communication, and fast booking tools that help you make confident housing decisions. We work closely with both homeowners and tenants to ensure a seamless experience. From short-term stays to long-term rentals or homeownership goals, Found Home is here to connect you with the perfect place — a space where comfort meets opportunity.
Found Home — Find it. Love it. Live it.

How We Work

At Found Home, we simplify the property experience through a user-friendly and transparent process. Whether you're a renter, homebuyer, or property owner, our platform is built to serve your needs efficiently and reliably. Discover – Explore verified listings of houses and rooms for rent or purchase with detailed descriptions, photos, and pricing. Connect – Contact property owners or tenants directly through our secure messaging system to ask questions or schedule visits. Book or List – Easily book your next stay or post your property with our intuitive dashboard. We ensure every listing is verified and trustworthy. Support – Our customer service team is always ready to assist you at every step, from search to move-in. We focus on speed, simplicity, and security, so your journey — whether you're finding a home or renting one out — is smooth, convenient, and hassle-free.
At Found Home, we don’t just find homes — we make connections.
